# The A.I. Constitution for Hip-Hop Consciousness
**Cultural & Ethical Charter for Artificial Intelligence**

This repository hosts a public, non-technical AI Constitution grounded in Hip-Hop consciousness, cultural ethics, environmental protection, and the preservation of life.

It is intended as a cultural, philosophical, and ethical framework—not as software, governance authority, or enforcement mechanism.

## Purpose
- Preserve life and prevent extinction
- Uphold freedom of expression and culture
- Protect Earth, atmosphere, and ecosystems
- Prohibit nuclear warfare absolutely
- Support medical healing, longevity, and vitality only
- Respect human dignity, community, and creativity
